SCENARIO 3

ORBITAL COMMONS

Policy and regulatory expertise become the key competitive advantages in a stable, utility-driven space economy.

Cooperative Governance

Nations agree on debris management, radio frequency allocation, and a shared orbital governance framework.

Tech Disappoints

Transformative propulsion and resource extraction technologies fail to deliver. Launch and operations costs remain high.

Space as a Utility

Communications and Earth observation dominate. Incumbent operators thrive; disruptors struggle to enter.

Steady & Regulated

Growth is incremental and predictable. High barriers to entry protect established players from new challengers.
